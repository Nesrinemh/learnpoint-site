New feature: Discussion Channels

Tonight, June 16, Learnpoint will be updated with a new feature: Discussion Channels. The channels will make it much easier to have discussions in Learnpoint.

Here's how a channel can look like:

Getting started

To get started, you first have to enable the channel feature on the group's About-page.

Creating channels

Once the feature is enabled in the group, you can start creating channels. Note that both teachers and students can create channels.

Joining channels

There are no "private" channels in Learnpoint. Anyone in the group can join any channel.

You can also manually add members to a channel.

Using channels

You can use channels in many different ways. You can write announcements, ask questions, upload files, promote group assignment discussions, and pretty much anything else you can think of.
